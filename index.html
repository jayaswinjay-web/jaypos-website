Frequently asked questions

Common questions about JayPOS.

Does JayPOS require internet to work?
No. JayPOS is built as an offline-first application. Billing, inventory management, reporting, and printing all work without any internet connection. Cloud sync happens automatically in the background when internet becomes available.
What printers are supported?
JayPOS supports any ESC/POS Bluetooth thermal printer. Most 58mm and 80mm thermal printers available in India work out of the box. The app auto-discovers paired Bluetooth printers.
Is my data safe and backed up?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device and automatically synced to Supabase cloud when online. The sync is encrypted and your data is accessible only to you via the admin dashboard.
Can I use JayPOS on multiple devices?
The Professional plan supports multi-user access. Each staff member can log in with their own credentials and access the same shop data. Changes sync across devices seamlessly.
How do I get started?
Download the APK from this website, install it on your Android device, and contact the admin team to set up your shop credentials. Once logged in, you can start adding products and billing immediately.
