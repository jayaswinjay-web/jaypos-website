Frequently asked questions

Common questions about JayPOS.

Does JayPOS require internet to work?
No. JayPOS is built as an offline-first application. Billing, inventory, reporting, and printing all work without any internet. Cloud sync happens automatically in the background when internet becomes available.
What printers are supported?
JayPOS supports any ESC/POS Bluetooth thermal printer. Most 58mm and 80mm thermal printers available in India work out of the box.
Is my data safe and backed up?
Yes. All data is stored locally and synced to Supabase cloud when online. The sync is encrypted. You can access your data via the admin dashboard.
Can I use JayPOS on multiple devices?
Yes. The Professional plan supports multi-user access. Each staff member logs in with their own credentials and accesses the same shop data.
How do I get started?
Download the APK, install it on your Android device, contact the admin team to set up your shop credentials. Start adding products and billing immediately.
